Kigali Chamber of Commerce - Site Plan

Site Name

Name: Kigali Chamber of Commerce

Reason: This name clearly identifies the organization’s mission to support businesses in Kigali and promote economic growth.

Optional Domain: kigalicoc.org

Site Purpose

The site will serve as the official information hub for the Kigali Chamber of Commerce, providing business resources, membership registration, event announcements, and contact details. It will also highlight local business opportunities and promote collaboration among Kigali entrepreneurs.

Scenarios

  • How can I become a member of the Kigali Chamber of Commerce?
  • When is the next business networking event in Kigali?
  • Where can I find contact details for the Chamber’s board members?

Color Scheme

Primary Color: Dark Green (#004d40) – used for headers, navigation background, and call-to-action buttons.

Secondary Color: Orange (#ff9800) – used for highlights, links, and icons.

Typography

Headings: Merriweather (serif) – to give a professional and authoritative tone.

Body Text: Open Sans (sans-serif) – for readability and a modern look.
